1- Analysis

The sole purpose of switching to a new information system is to create an information system that is more efficient, productive, traceable and produces reports that will inform decision-making mechanisms correctly. This requires a good understanding of the company's business processes, objectives and the challenges to be faced during the transition to the new system. In the "AYZ Methodology", understanding the needs, objectives and analyzing the existing structure are carried out in 3 steps.

  1. Management Reports (Top-down Approach)

    In the first step of the analysis phase, it is necessary to discuss with the company management their expectations from the information system and the reports they want what they are and whether they can be obtained, and if so the departments and methods by which they are obtained and is documented.

  2. Operational Operations (Bottom-up Approach)

    The organization established to carry out the day-to-day operations of the business system (manual and/or partial automation) and what work is done (work flow and processes), challenges encountered and solutions problem areas that need to be addressed are identified and documented It is done.

  3. Identification of Technical Assets

    What is the network, hardware and software status of the existing system, whether it can meet the objectives is measured and documented.

Estimated Duration: 1-2 Weeks

4- Transition to Training and Implementation

When a new system is introduced or when employees with resistances when their habits, their tools are changed. is a reality. The total number of employees of a company experience, wisdom and expertise are the sole determinants of a company's success. is the element.

The AYZ Methodology is a methodology that helps company employees to be eager to transition to the new system. to ensure that they are able to be a part of the design process. During the design phase, all users are consulted for their opinions, so users will be able to are willing to be active and voluntary users of the system.

Before the training, training groups are formed by taking into account the roles of individuals or departments. According to the training groups, AYZ experts prepare a "User Guide" that employees can refer to later. Employees are given comparative training in groups with reference to the "User Guide". Differences and conveniences between the new system and the old system are explained.

Estimated Duration: 1 Week
